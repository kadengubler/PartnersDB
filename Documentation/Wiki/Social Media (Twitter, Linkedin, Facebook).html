Posting to facebook 

Facebook Page: Partners in Business at Utah State University

 

The goal is to increase the number of followers to maintain an active audience that keeps updated with what's going on with PIB. To increase the number of fans or people who like the Page, you need to like other pages of interest to what partners have to offered.

 

Set up a target/goal in terms of number of fans and offer prizes/rewards when the goal is reached. This will work as an incentive to become a fan of our page (i.e. $35 blue cache card)
