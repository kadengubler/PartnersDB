Make a page for each seminar

Do this in a full screen EditGrid screen (push the downward facing arrow button in the upper left hand corner of the EditGrid, and select full screen)

  • Right click on the Coord. Template tab and copy the sheet.
  • Rename the tab for the seminar that this sheet will be for
  • DO NOT CHANGE OR DELETE THE Coord. Template sheet
  • Open the Task tab
  • Copy all of the tasks from column C to G and from the top of the coordinator tasks to the bottom
  • Paste the tasks into the A4 of the new sheet created
  • If there are not enough rows of equations drag the equations down from columns F through L
  • Hide Columns B through H
