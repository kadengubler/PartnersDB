Important Steps
 Key Points Reasons for Key Points

After doing a search or adding a new contact, click on the REGISTRATION button on their screen.

    
For a New Contact If the person is new to the database, this will be their first seminar to attend. You will need to add their Contact ID # (from the Contacts Form) into the Contact ID box. Either remember it, or move the Registration form out of the way to view it. Future seminar registrations for this person will automatically add the Contact ID.

 

 

 
For a Contact already in the database

1. If the person is already in the database and has attended a seminar, clicking on the LAST button (arrow pointing to |) will go to the last seminar added.

2. If necessary, check back one or two seminars to make sure they are not already registered as the seminars are not in alphabetical order.

3. If not already registered, or if new, click  the + sign to add them to a new seminar.

  
For both New Contacts and Contacts in the database

1. Choose the EVENT YEAR. A seminar series runs from September to May during the school year. The year the series ends is the EVENT YEAR. So if it is October 2008, the event year would be 2009, or 09 in the drop-down box.

2. Select the SEMINAR EVENT.

3. Click the appropriate box if they are attending 1 day or 2 days.

4. If the person is coming to 1 day of a 2-day seminar, in the Comments box type the day they are coming (ie: Wednesday only).

  

FOR PARTNERS 

If they are a Partner

1. On the registrations received via email, there is a spot where the registrants put their partnership #.

2. Put the partnership # in the PARTNERSHIP ID box. Be sure to include enough zeroes in the number to equal 5 places (ie: X00027), unless it is a Lifetime member, then only use 4 places (ie: LT0027).

3. Close the Registration Form.

  

FOR PARTNERS 

If they have a partnership, but are an Extra Attendee.

1.  Put the partnership # in the PARTNERSHIP ID box.

2. Click EXTRA ATTENDEE in the attendee type check box.

3. Go to QuickBooks and create an invoice for them.

4. Put the invoice # in the Comments box (ie: Inv. #2345).

5. Close the Registration Form.

  

FOR NON-PARTNERS

 

1. Click Non-Partner in the attendee type check box.

2. Go to QuickBooks and create an invoice for them.

3. Put the invoice number in the Comments box (ie: Inv. #2345).

4. Close the Registration Form.

  

FOR USU FACULTY/STAFF

1. Click the appropriate box for USU Faculty/Staff.

2. They will be billed only for lunch and the daybook after the seminar during the Seminar Audit.

3. Close the Registration Form.

  
STUDENT HOSTS, GUEST, OR SPEAKER

1. Click the appropriate box for Host, Faculty/Staff, Guest, or Speaker.

2. There is no charge for these people.

3. Close the Registration Form.

  
PROFESSIONAL ACHIEVEMENT RECIPIENT

1. Register the person if they plan to attend more than just the lunch where they receive the award. Otherwise just add them and their guests to the lunch count.

2. If they are attending the seminar, click Professional Achievement in the Attendee type.

3.  There is no charge for this person.

4. Close the Registration Form.

  
Finishing the Registration

1. Put some sort of identifying mark (like a large check mark) on their original paper registration.

2. It is helpful to write the Invoice # somewhere on the registration sheet so you know they have been invoiced if needed.

  This is a visual to quickly see who has been registered and, if needed, invoiced.
File the Registration

Place Registration Form in Registration Binder under the appropriate seminar in alphabetical order.

 Not necessary, but alphabetical order makes it easier to look their registration up if there is a question.
