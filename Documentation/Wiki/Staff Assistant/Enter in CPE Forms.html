 Scan original forms into computer

1.       To run the scanner from your computer, open Adobe Acrobat Professional (not Adobe Reader) from desktop.

2.       Click “Create PDF” button in top menu bar (or under “File), click “From Scanner” in drop down box.

3.       Click “Scan”.

4.       Find where you want to save the file, and give it a name.

5.       DO NOT CLICK “SAVE” quite yet.

6.       Place pages you need scanned in the copier automatic feeder (make your pile no larger than about ½” thick at a time).

7.       Now click “Save” on your computer.

8.       When the pile is done, put another pile in the feeder if needed.

9.       Go back to your computer.

10.   It will ask if you want to continue.

11.   Click OK.

12.   When you’re done, Adobe will take quite a while to format the pages into PDF.

13.   If you need to add more pages after the scanning,

14.   Open the scanned file that has the previously scanned pages.

15.   Click Create PDF/From Scanner, and under Output make sure “Append” is selected, with the correct file in the Browse window next to it.

16.   Click “Scan”, put the documents needing to be scanned in the copier.

17.   On the copier press the Scan button, make sure the “Online” button is selected.

18.   Once the documents are scanned, go back to your PDF file and make sure they were added at the end of the documents. It will take a few minutes for the documents to be added.

19.   Scroll up and find the spot where you will want to insert the form. Might want to write down the page numbers to put the new document between.

20.   Grab one of the newly scanned documents with your mouse, and while holding it, move the document up in between the pages to place it alphabetically.

21.   Repeat with the other forms.

22.   Save the document on the Z Drive under "Partners/Seminars/(the correct seminar" and the "correct year".
