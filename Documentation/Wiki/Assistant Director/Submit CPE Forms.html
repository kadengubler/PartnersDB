Important Steps
 Key Points Reasons for Key Points
Create application to submit to Utah CPE Registry
  • CPE forms can be found on the Z drive under Partners, Administration, Amy, CPE Certification, Click on the CPE Approval form from the previous year's seminar. 
  • Change the dates but keep close to the same format as the previous year.
 This application must be submitted to the Utah CPE Registry for the attendees to receive the CPE credits for the seminar.  The Utah CPE Registry will review the application and inform us if the application gets approved.
Gather Materials
  • Brochure
  • Daybook
  • Any additional handouts from speakers
  • Copy of CPE Form from Seminar
  • Copy of Evaluation from back of daybook for the seminar
  • Copy of Seminar attendees

 

 All of these materials must be included with the application to receive approval.
Submit application & Materials
  • Submit to the following address:
             Utah CPE Registry

             220 East Morris Avenue, Suite 320 

             Salt Lake City, UT 84115

  Once you receive confirmation that the CPE credits are approved for that seminar, send out an e-mail using the e-mail list the staff assistant creates to inform the attendees of the approval.

 Sign forms
  •  Sign your name and date the forms (if the forms are torn out of the daybook, it makes it easier to cut the edges using the paper cutter to get a straight edge)
  
 Give signed forms to Staff Assistant
  •  Staff assistant will create list of e-mails, scan in originals, and create mailing labels to mail out to each attendee receiving the credit.
  

Send out approval e-mail to attendees

Once you receive a letter in the mail saying it was approved, send out an e-mail to the attendees with the approval code.  

Example:
