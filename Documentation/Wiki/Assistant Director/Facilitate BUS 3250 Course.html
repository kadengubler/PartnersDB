Dear 3250 Students:

I want to remind you to attend the Operational Excellence Seminar this Wednesday & Thursday, September 16-17, 2009.  The schedule can be found by going to our website at www.partners.usu.edu

Click on Seminars, Operational Excellence, and then you can view the schedule as well as each speaker’s bio and presentation summary. The sessions will be held in at the Eccles Conference Center on the 2nd floor (main floor).  The keynote sessions will take place in room 216 and the breakouts will be in either 216, 205/207, or 201/203.  Remember you need to submit notes from 7 Partners in Business speakers as well as 3 deans convocations (September 25, September 30th, and November 18th).   Please submit your assignments on blackboard as soon as you have completed them. If you are unable to stay for an entire presentation, please be respectful of the other attendees and sit towards the back of the room and remember to leave quietly.  (Please note that Wednesday afternoon are 4 hour workshops.   Due to limited space, they are NOT open to students. Plan to attend any speakers you can during Wednesday morning, or throughout the day on Thursday.)

 

 

I also want to make a few things clear.  You do not need to register for the seminar, so come listen to the speakers you are interested in.   The breakfast and lunch are for paying attendees only.  Your tuition and class fees do not cover food at our seminars, they go directly to the School of Business.  Please be respectful of those who have paid for the seminar and do not take the food or break items.   Please let me know if you have any questions.

Thanks!
