 Update syllabus

 

  • Go to the Z drive under Administration/Amy and then click on the BUS 3250 folder.  Find the most recent folder that has a current syllabus (the previous semester).  
  • Save the syllabus with a new name for the current year.  
  • Update the seminar dates as well as the convocation dates on the syllabus.
  • Call Dean's Office 7-8328 to get dates for Dean's convocation to update in syllabus
  • Update the required book once the School of Business announces the new book for the year. (It's the same book the faculty is required to read for the Fall Retreat).
  
 Create course section on blackboard
  • Review FACT tutorials online  http://it.usu.edu/fact/htm/software-tutorials or schedule appointment to attend training workshop http://it.usu.edu/fact/htm/training-workshops
  • Log into bb.usu.edu
  •  Enter in your A-Number and strong password
  • Once you are logged in, go to the most recent section (there should be one that automatically populates for the new semester).
  • Click on the "Copy Content from another Course" button
  • Click Continue
  • Choose the most recent course to copy the information from.
  • Click on Course Content and then click on the syllabus button.
  • Select the syllabus you want to use for the semester (use your browse button and find the syllabus you updated.  The syllabus can be found on the Z drive under Administration/Amy/BUS3250 Course and then find the most recent folder. It should link it to what you chose.
  • Then go to the Assignments tab.  Update each assignment with the proper due date. (Usually everything is due the last Friday before finals week.)  Make sure you change the Book report instructions to the correct book for the semester.
  • If questions, contact Fact Administrator over the School of Business http://it.usu.edu/fact/htm/contact-us
    • 2010 contact is Shane Thomas 797-0525 or shane.thomas@usu.edu
  
 E-mail student
