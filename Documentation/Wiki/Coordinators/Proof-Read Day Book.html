Proofread daybook.
  • Look for errors in the body of all text, and in all speaker information.
    • Spelling errors
    • Spacing issues
    • Content/context errors
  • Make sure the slides are in the correct order
  • Any errors are distracting and minimize the credibility of the program.  The daybook needs to be very professional, clean and clear.
Ask other coordinators in the office to proofread.
  • Multiple sets of eyes will help ensure the document is error-free.
  • You are ultimately responsible for the professionalism and correctness of your seminar publications.
