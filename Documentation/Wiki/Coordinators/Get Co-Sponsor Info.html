Deadline: 15 Weeks Before Seminar

Concurrent Step(s): Get Speakers Info

Next Step(s): Proof Read Brochure

Previous Step(s): Confirm Co-Sponsors, Confirm Speakers

 

Important Steps
 Key Points Reasons for Key Points

Send email with deadlines
  • Co-Sponsor Deadlines.docx  should be the body of the email
  • Change dates to specific dates
  • Make sure the speakers know this email is coming, so they will open the attachment

 

 

  • Specific dates are much easier to understand than weeks or days before the seminar.
Attach Co-Sponsor Info form
  • There is an online version of the co-sponsor info from. Email a link to this form to your co-sponsors.
  • If the co-sponsor requests a PDF version of the form, then send then click HERE for a PDF version. 
  • This will get all of the information besides the picture and powerpoint slide from the co-sponsor and make it very easy for them.
Put the information in the Folders
  • Put all of the information received from the co-sponsors in a folder for that specific co-sponsor. Make sure to save the information as a PDF.
  • The folder should be in the z drive in the co-sponsor relations folder.
  • The folder should be the name of the organization co-sponsoring the event
  • Having standards between the coordinators helps the assistant director and the graphic designer to find the information they need
Follow up until you have the necessary info
  • Change formats, phone, email, and letters are all good ways of contacting
  • If letters are used, call to make sure they got it, and make it personalized
 
  • It is essential to follow up, people will forget about the deadlines, so remind them.  Different formats get more commitment
Give all info to graphic designer
  • Let coordinator know when new information is in your folder.
  • The designer will put this info in the brochure and daybook
Get Mailing Lists
  • This should be in excel or access format (If this isn't possible we need two sets of lables that are separated by a blank sheet of paper).
  • Email addresses are not necessary, however we will forward an email to the co-sponsors for them to forward to their members.
 
  • Electronic formats save time and money, because the printer will send them for us.
  • Email blasts save money as well by reducing the quantity of brochures that need to be printed.
 
Put cosponsor information on website (Refer to Standardized EZ Plug Manual for Exact instructions) 
  • Add Logo
  • Add Contact Information
  • Link to cosponsor's website EZ-Plug Posts.docx
  
Start using the itinerary once you get any information from your Co-Sponsors.
  • Use the Speaker Itinerary Database
  • This will save you a lot of time as you approach your seminar.
