Get updated list of Partners
  • Staff assistants will have these lists with all updated information.

 
Call your Partners
  • Each coordinator should have a list of Partners. Each Partnering organization should receive a reminder call for each seminar.
  • Target specific organizations that have a higher interest or relevance to the particular seminar.
  • Volunteer to set the contact up on our email list, or email out a brochure if they are interested
    • Ask them to pass along the information to anyone who might be interested.
  • This is a great way to inform and remind our Partners of our great, upcoming seminar.
  • Break down your list--try calling for 15 minutes each day until you have called your entire list.
To help continuously update our records, collect and report any information you receive while speaking with the contact.
Some examples include:
  • Was the actual contact person the same person listed on call sheet?
  • Which seminars did the contact express interest in?
  • We don't want to find the contact each time.

 

  • We want to match interests with seminars.  There might be a different contact in the same organization interested in other seminars.  We won't call for seminars they are not interested in.
Give data to Staff Assistant to update in the database
  • Any relevant information, including examples from above should be passed along to the Staff Assistant.
