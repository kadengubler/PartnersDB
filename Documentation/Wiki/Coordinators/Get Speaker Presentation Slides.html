Get presentation slides from the speakers
  • There is a deadline for sending in presentation slides on the original speaker deadline email.
  • With each email that you send out to speakers, remind them of upcoming and uncompleted deadlines.
  • Presentation slides should be sent and saved as .ppt, .pptx, or .pdf files.
  • If the speaker does not want them included/printed in the daybook, you don't have to get them
  • DON'T send MASS EMAILS to SPEAKERS!  Each speaker has different materials, reaches deadlines at different times, and needs individual attention.
  • Speakers must know the deadline in advance so they can prepare.
  • Again, speakers are busy.  It is helpful to send polite reminders about deadlines. 

 

  • These files are easy to insert and print in the daybook.

 

  • You take better care of your speakers as you pay attention to them individually. Happy speakers give referrals.. for future speakers and attendees.  Because it is a non-profit with a limited budget, Partners loves referrals!
**Note:  This is not as easy as it sounds, so be consistent and follow up with your speakers frequently.  
  • Because attendees love and specifically request speakers' slides, try to obtain as many presentations as you can.  (Some speakers will not allow us to publish presentation slides.  Please respectfully accommodate these requests.)
