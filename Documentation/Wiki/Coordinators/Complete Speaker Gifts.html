Purchase Gifts
  • Budget $15-20 per speaker
  • Good quality gifts are expected.
  • The gift must have either the USU or Partners in Business logo.
  • Possible gift vendors are listed below the grid
  • The hope is that speakers will keep and display the gift. 
  • The displayed gift lifts the brand of Partners in Business. 
Wrap Gifts
  • Wrap the gifts
    • Use USU paper, seals or stickers in the wrapping
  • Wrapping the gift is nice and professional. Using Utah State gift paper etc. is part of the branding. 
Give Gifts
  • Student hosts or staff will give gifts to the speakers.
  • Student hosts who have made a significant connection relationship with speakers should present the gift.  In the case that staff has built a stronger relationship, it is appropriate for them to present the gift. The gift-giving should be personal, an extension of a relationship or connection. It is an important part of conveying value and brand.
