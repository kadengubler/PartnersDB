All forms must be tailored to your specific seminar.  To do this open them from here, then save them in your own folder on the Z drive.  After saving them, modify them to suit your seminar.

 

Speaker and Host Relations

  • Itinerary Form.xlsx - Use the itinerary Form for all speaker and host tracking.  Conversation notes, addresses, phone numbers, AV needs, etc. (Instructions for this are here Itinerary Form Instructions.docx)
  • Speaker Deadlines.docx - Use the Speaker Deadlines form to send to the speakers so that they know when everything is due.  After modifying this document paste the contents into the body of your Speaker Deadlines email.
  • Presenter info form.doc - The Presenter Info Form will help you to get the information for the brochure, daybook, and website from your speaker.  After modifying this document (Modifying Word Forms)attach it to the Speaker Deadlines email.
  • Travel and Logistics Form.doc - The Travel and Logistics form is to obtain all of the information needed for hotel stays, flight times, AV needs etc.  After modifying this document (Modifying Word Forms) attach it to the Speaker Deadlines email.
  • Student Host Invite.doc - This is an example of an email blast that would be sent to all of the business students to invite them to be student hosts.
  • Student Hosts responsibilites.doc - This has our expectations for the student hosts.
  • Good example of an introduction OGIO Intro.docx.  You can give this to student hosts so they know what is expected of them.
  • Speaker Evaluations.xlsx - This is where the feedback info from the attendees is entered.
  • Evaluation Form (Speaker).doc - Send this form to the speakers after the seminar for them to evaluate the seminar, coordinator, and student host. This will help us to know how to improve these things in the future.
