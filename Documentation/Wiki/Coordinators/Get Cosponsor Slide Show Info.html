General Information on Slide Show
  • Partners runs a slide show in the main keynote room between presentations.
  • This gives attendees something to look at/pay attention to, and provides an advertising opportunity.

Get all slides from co-sponsors
  • By now, co-sponsoring organizations should have sent you their promotional slide.
  
Add any trivia or quotes about your seminar topic that you would like.
  • Trivia and quotes are a good way to keep attendees interested in the slide show
  • Trivia should focus on:
    • the seminar topic and speakers 
    • the Partners in Business program
    • Utah State
    • Logan
  • Attendees are more likely to watch the slide show if it captures their interest.  The more attendees watch the slide show, the more valuable it is as an advertising tool.
  • Each seminar will keep these standard focuses, creating consistent slide shows.
Include slides promoting upcoming seminars to the slide show.
  • A good rule of thumb is to use one slide per upcoming seminar.
  • Slides should be informative, interesting, and clear.  (Do not bog down with too much text.)
  • As this is a good promotional medium, we can use the slide show as a way to increase awareness, interest, and registration in upcoming seminars.
Give all info to Graphic designer
  • It is the job of the graphic designer to give the slide show a professional look and keep all slides consistent.
